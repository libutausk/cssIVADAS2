LOBSTER

Lobsters aren’t red. They turn red when cooked, but in nature they can be green or yellow or even bright blue.

Scientific name: Homarus gammarus

Lifespan: 45 - 50 years

Length: 25 -50 cm

Weight: Up to 20.15 kg

Lobsters are a family of large marine crustaceans. Lobsters have long bodies with muscular tails, and live in crevices or burrows on the sea floor.
